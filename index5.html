Help

Welcome to our platform, where we strive to simplify the printing process for everyone. Our mission is to bridge the gap between users and print shopkeepers by offering a seamless file upload and download service. Whether you're uploading important documents or downloading print-ready files, our platform ensures a hassle-free experience, making printing accessible to all.

How do I upload files?

To upload files, simply navigate to the "Upload" section and follow the instructions provided. You can select your files and upload them securely.

How can I download my files?

After uploading your files, you can download them by visiting the "Download" section. Simply select the files you uploaded and initiate the download process.
